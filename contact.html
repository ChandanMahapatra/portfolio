Overview

The At Home system can tie home appliances and electrical fixtures together so they perform as one unified digital system with the help of an intuitive user-interface. The process will be quick and simple. The aim of the system is to help users who might be intimidated by technology.

My Role

Interaction Design, User Research and Visual Design

Team

Chandan, Rootwa, Sangram and Trina

Tools

Illustrator, Invision and Photoshop

Problem

Home automation systems face four main challenges; these are the high cost of ownership, inflexibility, complex interface, and difficulty achieving security. The complex interface makes the system inaccessible and difficult to interact with people who might be technologically inept.

Solution

The At Home system will be able control the entire home environment, ranging from lighting, thermostat settings to audio-visual playback is placed at the hands of the user. Users who are not tech-savvy will be able to use this system since it will be heavily influenced by real world interactions.
